About XD Magazine

XD Magazine aims to share engaging Filipino stories that highlight challenges and successes in Filipino culture. Our goal is to entertain, inform, and inspire with narratives that connect people across the world.

Mission & Vision

Mission

Our mission is to create a go-to digital platform designed to ignite passion through Filipino storytelling and connect a global audience with Filipino experiences.

Vision

We envision XD Magazine as the leading digital platform for discovering Filipino narratives, where everyone can explore and celebrate Filipino culture.
